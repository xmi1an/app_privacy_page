Privacy Policy

BCA Practical does not require an account and does not collect or share personally identifiable information. It uses limited, anonymous analytics and fetches practical content over the network, as described below.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

BCA Practical ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. No account or sign-up is required, and we do not collect or share any personally identifiable information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable data.

  • No account or sign-up is required.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or files.
  • We use Firebase Analytics to understand anonymous, aggregate feature usage. This data is not tied to your identity.

How the App Works

Practical programs, source code, outputs, and explanations are fetched from a public, static JSON API hosted on GitHub and cached on your device, so the app keeps working offline between updates. The app uses the internet to:

  • Fetch and refresh practical content (code, output, explanations) from our GitHub-hosted JSON API.
  • Download fonts (Google Fonts) for display.
  • Report anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Analytics).

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to record anonymous, aggregate usage events such as app opens, practicals viewed, bookmarks and practiced status toggled, practicals shared, and explanations opened. These events are associated with an anonymous analytics identifier only — never with your name, email address, or any account.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts — serves the fonts used in the app.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous usage analytics. Google's Privacy Policy applies.
  • raw.githubusercontent.com (GitHub) — hosts the static JSON files used to deliver and update practical content.

Data Security

We do not collect personally identifiable data, so there is no identity-linked data for us to lose or misuse. Analytics data is managed securely by Firebase.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and on this page.
