Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Job Search Application ("App")

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: The email associated with your Google account
  • Name: Your display name from your Google profile
  • Profile Photo: Your Google account avatar (if available)

While using the app, we collect:

  • Saved Jobs: Jobs you like or bookmark
  • Device Token: FCM token for sending push notifications

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Identify and authenticate your account
  • Remember your saved jobs and preferences
  • Send push notifications about new job postings
  • Improve the app experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers (Supabase, Firebase) who process data on our behalf

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored:

  • Supabase: Account profile and saved jobs
  • Firebase: Push notification tokens

All data transmission is secured with HTTPS encryption.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: View the data collected about you
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications

To delete your account, sign out from the app or contact us at jadavmilan009@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
