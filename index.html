Privacy Policy

BCA Notes · Last updated May 2026

BCA Notes is a study-material reader for BCA students. It does not require an account and collects no personal information. This page describes everything the app touches.

No Account Required

There is no sign-up, no login, and no profile. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.

Study Materials

PDF notes and past papers are fetched from a public GitHub repository (xmi1an/bca). When you open a PDF, your device downloads it directly from GitHub over HTTPS. We do not track which materials you view.

On-Device Storage

Bookmarks, recently viewed PDFs, theme preference, and a local cache of the material index are stored only on your device. None of this data is uploaded to any server. Uninstalling the app removes everything.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data. The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics services, and no tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

BCA Notes is intended as an educational tool. Because the app collects no personal information and requires no account, it does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version is always available at this URL, and the "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact
