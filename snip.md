# Privacy Policy — Snip

_Last updated: 3 July 2026_

Snip is an offline-first notes app. Your privacy is the default, not a setting.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Snip has no account, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking SDKs. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data, and it has no servers to send anything to.

- All notes, debts and tasks (and any reminder times you set) are stored **only on your device**, in a local database.
- Snip itself makes **no network calls** to send your data anywhere.

## Voice input (microphone)

Snip has an optional **voice capture** button. When you tap the mic, Snip records audio only for that moment and passes it to **your device's own speech-recognition service** (for example, Google's on Android) to turn speech into text.

- Snip does not store recordings and does not send audio to us.
- Depending on your device and its settings, that system speech service **may process audio in the cloud**. That handling is governed by your device/OS provider's privacy policy, not Snip's.
- Voice input is entirely optional — if you never tap the mic, no audio is ever captured.

## Data you choose to share

- **Backup export**: when you export a backup, the app creates a plain JSON file and hands it to the Android share sheet. Where that file goes (Drive, email, another device) is entirely your choice and happens outside the app. Backup files are **not encrypted** — treat them like any private document.
- **Call / WhatsApp shortcuts**: tapping a contact action opens your dialer or WhatsApp. No data is sent by Snip itself.

## Permissions we use and why

| Permission | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Notifications | To show the reminders you set. |
| Microphone | For optional voice capture (speech-to-text), only while you are dictating. |
| Run at startup | To re-schedule your reminders after the phone reboots. |

## Data deletion

Deleting a note deletes it from the local database. Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device.

## Children

Snip does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if optional cloud sync is added), the change will be listed here and in the app's release notes before it takes effect.
